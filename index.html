OUR VALUES

As L&T Construction, the construction arm of L&T, we are India's largest construction organization and ranked among the world's top contractors. We contribute significantly to build the image and stature of L&T across the world and drive L&T's reputation as 'the builder of the India of the 21st century' by offering EPC solutions to execute large industrial and infrastructure projects from concept to commissioning on a turnkey basis with single-source responsibility. Our multiple businesses encompass distinct but complementary capabilities, that address different segments of infrastructure and industry.
